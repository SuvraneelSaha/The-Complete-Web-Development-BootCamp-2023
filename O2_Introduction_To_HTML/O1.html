What exactly is html

html stands for hypertext markup language

hypertext ->pieces of text that links , means that it links to other text documents or to other html files that is the meaning of hypertext if you click on one hyperlink it will take you to another page

Markup language -- markup of texts - example quotation , bold , italics , capital letters editors used to highlight the text to show importance to the reader

Markup is done through html tags -- example h1 to h6 , div , br

There are thousands of html tags , you only need to learn about the top html tags and forget about the rest .
